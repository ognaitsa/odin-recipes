Omurice

Description:

Amazingly good fried rice topped with an omelette and a ketchup drizzle

Ingredients:

For the fried rice:

  • ½ an onion
  • 1 boneless, skinless chicken thigh
  • 1 tablespoon of extra virgin olive oil
  • ½ a cup of frozen mixed vegetables
  • ⅛ tablespoon of kosher salt
  • 1 and ⅓ cups of uncooked sushi rice
  • 1 tablespoon of ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce

For the omelette:

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil
  • 6 tablespoons of shredded sharp cheddar cheese

Steps:

For the fried rice:

  1. Mince the onion finely into ⅛-inch pieces

  2. Dice the chicken into ½-inch pieces

  3. Heat the oil in a large pan over medium heat and saute the onions until tender

  4. Add the chicken and cook until it's no longer pink

  5. Add the mixed vegetables and season with salt and pepper

  6. Add the cooled rice and use a cutting motion with your spatula to break the clumps into small pieces

  7. Add the ketchup and soy sauce and stir to combine. Transfer the fried rice to a plate and wash the pan

For the omelettes: (made individually)

  1. Whisk 1 egg and 1 tablespoon of milk in a small bowl

  2. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in the pan over medium-high heat. Distribute the oil to make sure the surface of the pan is coated well

  3. When the pan is hot, pour the egg mixture and tilt the pan to coat the entire surface with the egg. Cook the egg until the bottom has set but the top is still soft. Lower the heat to medium low.

  4. Sprinkle 3 Tbsp cheese on top, then add half of the ketchup fried rice across the middle of the omelette

  5. Use the spatula to fold both sides of the omelette over the middle to cover the fried rice. Carefully move the omurice to the edge of the pan

  6. Holding a plate in one hand and the pan in the other, flip the pan to transfer the omurice, seam side down, onto the plate
