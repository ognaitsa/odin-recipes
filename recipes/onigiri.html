Onigiri

Description:

Stuffed rice balls with a variety of fillings.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups of uncooked sushi rice
  • 5 and ½ cups of water
  • ¼ teaspoon of salt
  • ¼ cup of bonito shavings (dry fish flakes)
  • 2 sheets of nori (dry seaweed), cut into ½-inch strips
  • 2 tablespoons of sesame seeds

Steps:

  1. Wash rice in a mesh strainer until water runs clear. Transfer rice into a pot and combine with 4 and ½ cups of water. Bring water to a boil over high heat stirring occasionally. Reduce heat to low and cover. Simmer rice until water is absorbed. Allow the rice to cool.

  2. Combine the remaining cup of water with the salt in a small bowl. Use this water to dampen your hands and handle the rice. Divide the rice into equal portions and use one portion per onigiri.

  3. Divide one portion into two and gently make a dimple, fill with a big teaspoon of bonito flakes. Cover with the remaining rice and gently press into a triangle shape.
