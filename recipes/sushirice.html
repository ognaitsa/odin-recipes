Sushi Rice

Description:

Delicious sushi rice for all the rolls you want to make at home.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of uncooked sushi rice
  • 2 and ¼ cups of water
  • 1 quarter cup seasoned mirin

Steps:

  1. Wash rice in a mesh strainer with cold water until the water runs clear. Transfer to a pot and fill with 2 and ¼ cups of water.

  2. Place pot over medium-high heat and bring to simmer without stirring. When bubbles appear bring to low heat and cover. Cook for 10 minutes or until there is no more water. Remove from stove.

  3. Transfer the rice to a rimmed sheet pan. Pour the Seasoned Mirin and mix it using a WOODEN spoon. Let the rice cool down, you can use a fan or magazine to speed it up.
